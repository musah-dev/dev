From the source of the Nile to mountain gorilla trekking, Uganda offers unparalleled wildlife experiences and breathtaking landscapes that will leave you in awe.

Why Visit Uganda?

Gorilla Trekking

Experience the thrill of encountering mountain gorillas in their natural habitat at Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, home to nearly half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas.

Learn More

The Mighty Nile

Discover the source of the world's longest river at Jinja, where you can enjoy white-water rafting, kayaking, or a serene boat cruise along this historic waterway.

Explore Activities

Diverse Wildlife

Uganda's 10 national parks protect an incredible array of wildlife including the Big Five, chimpanzees, and over 1,000 bird species - a true birdwatcher's paradise.

View Safaris

Must-Visit Destinations

Bwindi Impenetrable NP

Southwestern Uganda

UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for mountain gorilla trekking in its ancient rainforest.

Murchison Falls NP

Northwestern Uganda

Where the Nile explodes through a narrow gorge creating one of Africa's most powerful waterfalls.

Queen Elizabeth NP

Western Uganda

Diverse ecosystems including savanna, forests, and lakes with tree-climbing lions and abundant wildlife.
