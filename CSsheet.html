What to Expect in Uganda

Uganda is a country with a myriad of impressive features. We have the best climate anyone could wish for. This is what is found in Uganda:

Physical Features

From the snow-capped Rwenzori Mountains to the vast Lake Victoria, Uganda's landscape will take your breath away.

Source of the Nile

Visit Jinja, the adventure capital, where the Nile begins its 6,650 km journey to the Mediterranean Sea.

Wildlife and Nature

10 national parks protecting lions, elephants, gorillas, and over 1,000 bird species await your discovery.

Important Travel Tips for Uganda

Beware, never go to a policeman looking for help: ever. Go to these places:

Hospitals
  • Mulago National Referral Hospital
  • Nsambya Hospital

These hospitals provide reliable medical services for travelers. Always have travel insurance.

Markets

Local markets are great for:

  • Finding authentic crafts
  • Trying local foods
  • Getting directions
  • Experiencing Ugandan culture

In Uganda, we have what we refer to as BABWONGO. They are often alongside roads; never interact with them.
